Pro Motion Marine Services


Pro Motion Marine provides professional marine rescue and salvage services, including recovery of sunken boats, vessels that have run aground or struck submerged obstacles, and the recovery of marine and industrial equipment. Pro Motion Marine offers Dock construction and dock installation. A vast array of equipment is stocked in house including The company has extensive experience working alongside the Canadian Coast Guard, insurers, and marine surveyors, with a 100% recovery success rate to date. Operations are conducted using certified vessels, specialized lifting equipment, and advanced sonar technology, prioritizing safety and damage mitigation.

Film Production Services


Michael Martin and Pro Motion Marine have provided marine support and safety coordination for major film productions, including:


  • 2000 The Whole Nine Yards
  • 2003 Timeline
  • 2008 Journey to the Center of the Earth
  • 2013 White House Down
  • 2014 X‑Men: Days of Future Pas
  • 2019 X‑Men: Dark Phoenix
  • 2023 Transformers: Rise of the Beasts

Services include picture boats, camera platforms, marine safety coordination, helicopter and water-based stunt support, construction and implementation of marine based sets and mechanisms, and emergency response planning for complex filming environments.

Fire Safety Services


Michael Martin provides professional fire safety and production safety consulting for film and television productions. With extensive experience in emergency response, marine safety, and on-set risk management, services include safety planning, emergency coordination, and operational decision support to ensure safe working environments both on water and on land.

About Michael Martin

Michael Martin is a marine safety professional with extensive experience in marine rescue, film production support, and emergency response. His background as a licensed paramedic and long-standing collaboration with major organizations makes him a trusted authority in high-risk operations.
