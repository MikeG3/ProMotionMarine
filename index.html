Pro Motion Marine Services


For over 25 years, Pro Motion Marine has provided professional marine rescue and assistance services, including recovery of sunken boats, vessels that have run aground or struck submerged obstacles. The company maintains a vast array of equipment is stocked in house including lift bags, multiple pumps, rigging and anchors, The company has extensive experience working alongside the Canadian Coast Guard, insurers, and marine surveyors, with a 100% recovery success rate to date. Operations are conducted using certified vessels, specialized lifting equipment, and advanced twin side scan sonar technology, prioritizing safety and damage mitigation.

Michael Martin provides professional production safety consulting including marine, fire, explosives and vehicle rescue for film and television productions. With extensive experience in emergency response, marine safety, and on-set risk management, services include safety planning, emergency coordination, and operational decision support to ensure safe and compliant working environments both on water and on land.

About Michael Martin


Michael Martin is a marine safety professional with decades of experience in marine rescue, film production support, and emergency response. He has worked extensively alongside the Canadian Coast Guard and has a background as a etired paramedic with experience in advanced care environments. His career bridges marine operations, film production logistics, and safety coordination, making him a trusted resource for complex and high-risk operations. His company has provided motorsports medical and rescue services for a legacy racetrack in Mont Tremblant for over 40 years.
