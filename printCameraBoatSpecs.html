Camera Boat

Transport Canada certified production support vessel designed for film production, marine coordination, camera crane operation, and water-based filming logistics.

Marine Specifications

  • Marine VHF radio with Mayday function
  • Draft: 26 inches
  • Air Draft: 8 ft (to top of GPS)
  • GPS chart plotter
  • Deck Capacity: subject to increase 6000 lbs
  • Maximum Passengers: 12
  • Minimum Crew: 2 (Min 1 SVOP + MED + MROC + First Aid)
  • Fuel tanks: 25 liters
  • Fuel: Premium 92+ Octane

Production Specifications

  • Transport Canada certified for 12 passengers plus crew
  • Safety equipment exceeds Transport Canada requirements
  • Custom tri-axle trailer with wide-load & repair equipment (banners, lights, flags, spare tire, jack and tools)
  • Boat Weight: 4600 lbs
  • Trailer Weight: 4200 lbs
  • Trailer Width empty or with boat: 12 ft
  • Trailer Length Overall: 40 ft
  • Trailer Height with Boat: 12 ft
  • Trailer-mounted 9000 lb winch
  • Tool box: Tongue mounted-lockable

Grip & Camera Specifications

  • Six Wichard 6mm folding pad eyes for crane mounting
  • Four Wichard 8mm folding pad eyes mounted on pontoons for crane lift/launch
  • (2510 WL) hard points for up to SuperTechno 30.  Minimum 24 inches clear to walk around camera crane
  • Capacity (subject to increase) 6000 lbs
  • Speed of boat loaded  = 9 Kts
  • 8 pipe clamps are required to facilitate Spud anchors in calm shallow water (max 9 ft). Aluminium pipes and clamps are Production supplied.
  • All pipe stanchions are removable and are compatible with typical 1.5 inches speed rail pipe
  • Deck Dimensions: 23 ft × 11 ft (253 sq ft) exclusive to production
  • Ramp access installable on bow or either side with 24 inches walking area.
  • Pilot/boat crew station elevated 24 inches to facilitate safe traveling shots
